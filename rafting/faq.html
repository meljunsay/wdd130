Frequently Asked Questions

We provide river rafting adventure experience year-round, but if you are a beginner, the best time to book your adventure is around January to May when Davao river is more friendly to beginners. If you have previous experience or a pro and looking for a more challenging rafting experience, the best times to book is during June to August.
