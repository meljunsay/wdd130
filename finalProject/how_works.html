How It Works

Step 1. Tell us about your home repair/improvement needs by clicking "Post Project" and filling up the Project Form.
Step 2. In a few seconds, see a list of local contractors. Compare service rates and choose the best contractor for your project. Contractor availability will also be shown at this stage as well as contractor performance rates.
Step 3. Wait for confirmation of project acceptance from the contractor. At this point, contractor will also send contract terms.
Step 4. Upon completion of your project, rate the performance of the contractor.
