River Rafting


If you are looking for a unique outdoor activity away from the noise and traffic of the city life, our river rafting adventure is perfect for you. Beginners will be given a workshop on safety procedures to ensure a fun and secure adventure for you. You may opt to do this fun activity with your own set of friends or meet new ones in the site. Our rafting packages include transfer from the city to the rafting area, rental of equipment like helmets, floatation devices, paddles and rafting boats. You will also be accompanied by our experienced rafting guides.


Book Rafting Adventure




Mountain Hiking


If you're not up for a river rafting adventure, we also provide guided mountain hiking trips where one of our trusted hiking experts will accompany your group as you explore the breath-taking Mount Apo traills and peak. We provide a one-day training class for beginners to learn the basics of mountain hiking safety and skills. Message us for more info.


Book Hiking Adventure



Camping


Let chirping birds and warbling brooks wake you up in our campsites and connect with nature on deeper level by sleeping among the trees, by the mountain and stream. Go solitary or camp with family or friends and let the singing crickets, beautiful night sky and cool forest breeze take away your worries and stress. You may bring your own camping gear or contact us to rent tents and other equipment. What are you waiting for, book your camping adventure now!

Book Camping Adventure



Fishing


Nothing like fishing to bring you peace and calm. Unplug while bonding with your family and friends in our fishing camps. Improve your concentration and patience while you relax by the river bank or on a boat while you wait for your catch. Fishing gear like hook, bait, rods and others are available for rent.
